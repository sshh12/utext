Legal Services for Students provides free legal advice to currently enrolled UT Austin students. We provide consultations on legal issues such as: Landlord/Tenant and Renting Criminal Charges Traffic Tickets Consumer Issues Car Wrecks Employment Law There are some things we do not provide consultations on. See our excluded services. Excluded Services Advising on the Affordable Care Act (a.k.a. "Obamacare") Auto Repairs Complex wills/wills involving property over $1,000,000 Disputes against any of the University of Texas units or entities Disputes between currently enrolled UT Austin students Immigration matters Legal questions pertaining to a matter that is not yours LSS cannot advise you on criminal matters similar to, or the same as, a matter which LSS previously has advised you.
