Women's Health Information: (512) 475-8242 Location: SSB 3rd Floor Make an online appointment Information on Charges and Insurance Hours: Monday - Friday 8:00am - 5:00pm Closed Saturday and Sunday and UT Holidays Wednesdays CLOSED 12pm - 1:30pm Semester hours exceptions In the UHS Womens Health Clinic, our certified nurse practitioners and registered nurses provide care by appointment. Womens Health welcomes all students who identify as women and all students who were assigned female at birth. You can make an appointment at UHS online or by calling 512-471-4955. UHS Women's Health Clinic offers: Routine Annual Wellness Exams Contraception advice, prescriptions and administration (pill, shot, ring, IUDs, implant, diaphragm) Sexually transmitted infection (STI) testing and treatment, including HIV testing Care for menstrual concerns (cramps, irregular bleeding, PMS, other related problems) Abnormal pap evaluation, including colposcopy Pregnancy testing and referrals Breast health assessment Evaluation and treatment for other common gynecological health issues such as: Vaginal infections Urinary tract infections Emergency contraception advice by phone. Emergency contraception is available over the counter at drugstores in Texas and sold at the Forty Acres Pharmacy. There are no age or gender restrictions for emergency contraception. VisitHealthy Sexualityfor more information about sexual health, annual exams, birth control methods, or any of the health issues listed above. Women's Health healthcare providers will refer patients to appropriate community resources for services not available at UHS. Due to high demand, please schedule routine annual exams at least two weeks in advance.
